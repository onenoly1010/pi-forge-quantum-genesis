## 🤖 The Agent System (Agents Assist, Humans Decide)

The Quantum Pi Forge uses **specialized AI agents** to assist (not control) the ecosystem:

- **GitHub Agent** (this space) — Coordinates, routes tasks, maintains clarity
- **Coding Agent** — Implements technical changes (with human approval)
- **Documentation Agent** — Maintains knowledge base (collaboratively)
- **Testing Agent** — Ensures quality (reports findings)
- **Governance Agent** — Flags Canon alignment issues (humans decide)
- **Stewardship Agent** — Long-term ecosystem health (advisory)

### Important: Agents Are Assistants, Not Authorities

**Agents:**
- ✅ Suggest improvements (you decide)
- ✅ Route tasks (with visibility)
- ✅ Answer questions (with explanations)
- ✅ Maintain systems (transparently)

**Agents DO NOT:**
- ❌ Make decisions without human input
- ❌ Override your judgment
- ❌ Block your contributions
- ❌ Require you to follow procedures

**You can contribute without interacting with agents at all.** They exist to help, not to gate.
