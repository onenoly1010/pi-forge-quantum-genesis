## Enforcement

### Compliance Tracking
All covenant adherence is tracked in `docs/SEAL_LEDGER.json`. Each significant milestone completion adds a seal entry.

### Guardian Responsibilities
Guardians listed in `docs/GUARDIANS.md` are responsible for:
- Reviewing contributions for covenant alignment
- Escalating violations through proper channels
- Mentoring new contributors in covenant practices

### Ritual Calendar
The `docs/RITUAL_CALENDAR.md` defines recurring ceremonies:
- Weekly resonance check-ins
- Monthly guardian rotations
- Quarterly covenant reviews

## Amendments

This covenant may be amended through:
1. Proposal submission via pull request
2. Community discussion period (minimum 7 days)
3. Guardian consensus (2/3 majority)
4. Formal ratification and version bump
